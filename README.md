Paw Plan: Agribusiness Management, BS — Interactive Degree Planner

Interactive drag-and-drop 4-year degree planner for NC State's Agribusiness Management, BS program in the College of Agriculture & Life Sciences.

## Features

- **Visual 4-year layout** — all 8 semesters + 4 summer sessions at a glance, color-coded by requirement category
- **Drag-and-drop scheduling** — move courses between semesters with real-time prerequisite enforcement
- **Course pickers** — click elective slots to browse and select from approved course lists (Humanities, Social Science, Natural Science, Technical Electives, Departmental Electives, and more)
- **Transfer credit** — mark courses as transferred (AP/IB/transfer) to see how your remaining semesters lighten up
- **Verify requirements** — automatic checking of GEP Global Knowledge and Foundations of American Democracy overlay requirements
- **Validation** — check for prereq violations, overloaded semesters, and total credit count
- **Save & load** — download your customized plan as a JSON file and reload it later
- **Undo** — reverse any action (picks, drags, transfers) up to 30 steps

## How to Use

Open `index.html` in any web browser — no installation or server required. See the built-in instructions panel at the top of the page for a full walkthrough.

## Important

This planner is an advising tool, not an official university system. Always confirm your plan against your **Degree Audit** and consult with your advisor before registering.
