ⓘ How to Use Paw Plan — Click to expand/collapse

Paw Plan helps you figure out when to take each course and in what order. Your Degree Audit is the official checklist of what you need — this tool helps you plan how to get there. Use both together.

📄 Upload Degree Audit
Start here. Upload your Degree Audit PDF from MyPack Portal and Paw Plan auto-fills completed courses into their correct slots, marks what's still needed, and organizes everything by semester.
➀ Pick courses
Dashed border = click to choose from approved lists.
➁ Drag to rearrange
Drag courses between semesters. Prereq violations are blocked automatically.
➂ Transfer credit
Click Transfer Mode, then click courses you've completed. They move to the bottom.
⚙ What If?
See how credit load changes affect your graduation timeline. Adjust with sliders.
🎓 Minor Explorer
See what you need for an Economics minor and add courses directly to your plan.
🖨 Advising Prep
Generate a printable summary to bring to your advising appointment.
💾 Save / Load
Save your plan as a file. Load it later or email it to your advisor.

↶ Undo reverses any action (up to 30 steps). Validate Plan checks for prereq issues. Reset restores the default plan.
⚠ This is an advising tool, not an official university system. Always verify against your Degree Audit.
